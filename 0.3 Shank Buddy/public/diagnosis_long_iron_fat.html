Chunking Long Irons

This is almost always due to weight transfer and/or poor wrist movement. Try the following:

  • Get your weight forward, chuncking your long irons usually starts with keeping your weight back.
  • Make sure you are hinging your wrists. Fixing this usually leads to a flush feeling contact.
  • Try thinking about a clockwise wrist movement in transition to create lag.
  • Pull those hands down and through (to the left) at impact. Get that front backhand feeling like it's scraping the ground.
