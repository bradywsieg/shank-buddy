Driver Slice

Something is causing you to come over the top. Knowing you, it's likely due to one of the following swing faults:

  • Not transfering weight forward properly. Make sure you get your weight forward in transition, especially late in the round when your legs are tired.
  • Opening/firing upper body too early. Don't get excited and flail open from the top. Be patient and wait for the club to drop into the slot before turning your shoulders towards the target. *remember, power doesn't come from the top - you have to "catch" your hands on the way down.
  • Not clearing your left hip. Make sure to aggressively clear your left hip in the downswing.
