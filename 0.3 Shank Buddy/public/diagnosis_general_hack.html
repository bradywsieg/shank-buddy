Playing like a hack.

You're stone cold sober and you're still hacking it. Your swing feels held together by duct tape. Try a few of the following thoughts and mini drills to snap out of it on the course:

  • Try hitting a few shots with a deliberately soft swing.
  • Really exaggerate your left hip clear and/or your shoulder rotation on the backswing.
  • Lets your hands drop before starting your shoulder movement in the downswing.
  • Accelerate through your short irons, make a shorter backswing.
  • Grip everything, including your putter, softer.
