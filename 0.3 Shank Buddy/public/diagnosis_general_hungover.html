Playing Hungover

You're on a boys trip and it's saturday morning after drinking 8 hazy's and 4 whiskey pickle backs. You can still play a good round of golf, just make sure to avoid the following hangover pitfalls:

  • Let your arms drop from the top of your backswing. Don't flail open immediately.
  • You tend to stand tall and swing flat. Don't be afraid to feel more "over" the ball at address.
  • Make sure to transfer your weight back and then forward.
  • Speaking of which, you tend to reverse pivot. Don't do that.
  • Don't sway on your backswing. Focus on making zero backwards sway.
  • make sure you aren't falling onto your toes during the swing.
