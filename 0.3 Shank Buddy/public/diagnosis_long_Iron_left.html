You are very close to a good swing - don't get discouraged. Knowing you, one of the following issues is likely plaguing you:

  • Be patient. Let the club drop into the slot. You are getting eager and firing your upper body too soon.
  • It's possible you have the ball too far forward. Trying inching it backwards.
  • Is your grip too strong? Trying weakening it slightly and see if that helps.
  • Swinging too hard. Slow it down , nice and easy.
