Hybrid/Wood Left

Missing left is not a bad miss for you! You're very close to a powerful draw - just need some small adjusting. Consider any of the following:

  • Opening/firing upper body too early while keeping the face square. compensating for this fault with the club face will cause a slice. However, keeping the face square with this swing fault will cause a ripper pull hook. Be more patient and wait for the club to drop into the slot before turning your shoulders towards the target. *remember, power doesn't come from the top - you have to "catch" your hands on the way down.
  • Move the ball slightly back in your stance. Sometimes this is the most simple and quickest fix.
