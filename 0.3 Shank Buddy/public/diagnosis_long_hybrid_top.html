Hybrid/Wood Top

Topping is super frustrating but luckily it's a pretty simple fix. Consider any of the following:

  • Stay down/keep your posture through impact. These clubs are lighter and it's easy to stand up when you try to rip it.
  • Swing easy. Don't try too hard to mash your hybrids.
  • Move the ball slightly back in your stance. Sometimes this is the most simple and quickest fix.
