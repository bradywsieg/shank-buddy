Putter Basics

Putting feels all fucked up? Try to get back to the basics, the checkpoints below help you putt like a maniac:

  • Tuck your elbows to your sides.
  • Soft grip, as soft as possible.
  • Bend over, see over the ball.
  • Overlap grip + tuck your right index finger.
  • Try reverse overlap if you want to switch things up (left hand fingers under)
  • Taking back with your hands has been preferable in the past, but if you're really struggling just take back with shoulders to keep it all together. Especially on short putts.
